02 · What this is

Three things at once.

Cosmo

A small green being who lives on your screen. He wanders, sits, gets distracted, sleeps if you leave him alone, gets curious if you tap. He started as a memory from 1992 — painted thirty years later, in watercolor, in the shape of that memory.

The Universe

The runtime, the first world, and the invitation. A Three.js companion-AI rig, a watercolor mushroom forest as the entry-Universe, and a documented contract anyone can implement. Universes plug in. Cosmo travels. State persists.

The invitation

Open, MIT, multilingual, authored by people who pair with Claude. Not a platform with a TOS. Not a marketplace. An open repository with clear documents — the repository is the project.

05 · Brand contract

The visual language is locked.

Universes that intentionally deviate can document their reasoning. Reviewers will engage seriously with the argument.

  • Visual baseHayao×Moebius watercolor; mushroom-cream, moss-sage, sky-wash, faded-rose, ink-aubergine, saffron-glow, forest-deep. Pop-accents capped at 5%.
  • VoicePoetic but grounded. The 1992 origin is referenced obliquely, never explicitly. Cormorant Italic for poetic copy, Inter for body.
  • NeverEmojis, placeholder graphics, standard-stock visuals, always-on trippy chaos. The world breathes; it doesn't shake.
  • Deviation policyBrand-locks change only via a Pivot Ledger entry in NORTH-STAR.md. Universes may deviate intentionally — document the reasoning in your README.
